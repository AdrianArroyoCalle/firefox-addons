Save to G. Drive

Description

Instant save your links on the internet to Google Drive

There are 2 modes of work:
* Firefox & Fennec: Save online files directly to Google Drive
* Thunderbird & SeaMonkey: Open Google Drive in a new tab

Manual

Firefox & Fennec

Right click (or long touch) on a direct link to a file. Click the option called "Save to Drive"

Thunderbird & SeaMonkey

Right click on the webpage or in message
